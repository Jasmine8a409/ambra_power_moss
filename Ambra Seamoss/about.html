About Ambra

Hi, I’m Ambra — the heart and hands behind Ambra’s Power Moss. My wellness journey started when I was searching for natural ways to boost my energy, support my immune system, and feel good in my own skin.

I fell in love with sea moss because it’s simple, powerful, and versatile. After months of learning how to properly clean, soak, and prepare it, I began sharing small batches with friends and family. Their results and feedback inspired me to turn this passion into Ambra’s Power Moss.

Every product is made with intention — from sourcing wild-crafted sea moss to carefully preparing it in my kitchen. My goal is to offer clean, wild, and revitalizing sea moss that fits easily into your daily routine.
